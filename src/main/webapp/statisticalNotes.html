Links

Certain data in the AMIS policy database were retrieved from existing WTO or OECD databases. In particular, all data on import tariffs, tariff quotas, export subsidies and in-quota tariffs come directly from WTO databases and can be accessed through the Tariff Analysis Online facility. The selected data on producer and consumer support originate from OECD’s Producer and Consumer Support Estimates database. Data on export restrictions are based on OECD’s Inventory of Restrictions on Exports of Raw Materials. Links to these databases and their detailed methodology documents can be found below:

- Tariff Analysis Online facility (WTO) . This facility allows you to access the WTO's Integrated Data Base (IDB) and Consolidated Tariff Schedules (CTS) database on-line
- Inventory of Restrictions on Exports of Raw Materials (OECD) . This inventory contains information on export regulation in the raw materials sector, namely in agricultural products, minerals, metals and wood
- Producer and Consumer Support Estimates database (OECD) . Policy data and indicators on government support to agriculture.
